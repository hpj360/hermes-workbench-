# Product Manager Skills

PM skill for Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, and Windsurf. Diagnoses SaaS metrics, critiques PRDs, plans roadmaps, runs discovery, coaches PM career transitions.

## Core Capabilities

### SaaS Metrics Diagnosis
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) analysis
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) forecasting
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) calculation
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) optimization
- Churn rate analysis and reduction strategies
- Net Dollar Retention (NDR) tracking
- Gross Margin and Unit Economics assessment
- Product-market fit indicators

### PRD Critique & Improvement
- Requirements clarity and completeness review
- User story acceptance criteria evaluation
- Edge case and error handling assessment
- Technical feasibility analysis
- Success metrics definition
- Dependency and risk identification
- UX/UI considerations

### Roadmap Planning
- Strategic alignment with business goals
- Feature sequencing and dependencies
- Resource capacity planning
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Milestone and KPI setting
- Stakeholder communication plans

### Product Discovery
- User interview question design
- Customer pain point identification
- Problem-solution fit validation
- Market research frameworks
- Competitive analysis methodologies
- Hypothesis formulation and testing

### Career Coaching
- PM to Director transition guidance
- Director to CPO development path
- Skill gap assessment
- Leadership development strategies
- Interview preparation
- Performance review guidance

## Key SaaS Metrics (32+)

### Growth Metrics
- MRR, ARR, Bookings
- New MRR, Expansion MRR, Contraction MRR
- Customer Count, New Customers, Logo Churn
- Trial Conversion Rate, Activation Rate

### Retention Metrics
- Gross Churn, Net Churn
- NDR (Net Dollar Retention)
- GRR (Gross Dollar Retention)
- Logo Retention, Revenue Retention
- Cohort Analysis

### Efficiency Metrics
- CAC Payback Period
- LTV:CAC Ratio
- Magic Number
- Rule of 40
- Gross Margin, Contribution Margin
- Operating Margin

### Product Metrics
- DAU/MAU Ratio
- Time in App, Session Length
- Feature Adoption Rate
- Time to Value
- Activation Funnel Conversion
- NPS, CSAT

## When to Use

- When analyzing SaaS business performance
- When reviewing or writing PRDs
- When planning product roadmaps
- When conducting product discovery
- When coaching PM career development
- When making data-driven product decisions
- When preparing for product interviews
