# AIPM News Digest

AI Product Manager daily intelligence digest. Fetches news from 16+ curated RSS sources across tech media, AI labs, research papers, developer communities, and more.

## Core Capabilities

### News Aggregation
- Curated RSS feed monitoring (16+ sources)
- AI and machine learning news tracking
- Product management insights
- Tech industry trends
- Startup and innovation updates

  Summary: 1-2 sentences on why this matters for PMs
  Key Takeaway: Actionable insight for product strategy
