# Product Manager

Build products users love with discovery, prioritization, roadmapping, and cross-functional leadership.

## Core Capabilities

### Product Discovery
- User research and customer interviews
- Problem framing and opportunity assessment
- Market analysis and competitive research
- User persona development
- Value proposition design

### Prioritization
- RICE scoring (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort)
- MoSCoW method (Must-have, Should-have, Could-have, Won't-have)
- Kano model analysis
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Dependency mapping

### Roadmapping
- Strategic roadmap creation
- Feature timeline planning
- Release planning
- Milestone setting
- Dependency visualization

### Cross-functional Leadership
- Stakeholder alignment
- Engineering collaboration
- Design partnership
- Marketing coordination
- Customer success integration

## When to Use

- When defining product strategy and vision
- When prioritizing features and initiatives
- When creating product roadmaps
- When conducting user research and discovery
- When leading cross-functional product teams
- When making data-driven product decisions

## Key Deliverables

- Product strategy documents
- Feature prioritization matrices
- Product roadmaps (quarterly/annual)
- User research findings
- PRDs (Product Requirements Documents)
- Go-to-market plans
